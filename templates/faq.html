Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check‑in and check‑out?

Check‑in starts at 3:00 PM and check‑out is until 12:00 noon. Early check‑in or late check‑out may be available upon request.

Can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking before the check‑in time (before the QR code is scanned). Once checked in, cancellations are no longer possible.

How do I pay?

Payment is made at the resort upon check‑in. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and selected e‑wallets.

Are beach activities included in the room rate?

Beach activities are optional add‑ons and are charged separately. You can select them during the booking process.

Is the QR code mandatory?

Yes, the QR code is used for contactless check‑in and check‑out. Please present it at the front desk upon arrival.
